#Project Scope
The scope of this project includes and excludes the following items.

## In scope
* Features
* Requirements
* Design Specification
* Life Cycle Process, pre-study, analyse, architecture, design, testing
* Organisation, Design, Quality Assurance

## Out of scope
* Business Analyze
* Business Requirement
* Organization, Business development, Support
* Life Cycle Process, marketing, deployment, support

## Deliverables
*	Feature specification
*	Requirement specification
*	architecture/design specification
*	Front-End web base UI
*	Design documentation
*	Test Case specification
*	Project Status Reports, will be extracted from Jira with interval defined by the steering committee
